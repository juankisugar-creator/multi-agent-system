### Stage 3: Trial â†’ Paid Conversion

**Free Tier Strategy (Choose One):**

**Option A: Time-Limited (RECOMMENDED)**
- Free: 14-day full access trial
- Why: Creates urgency, users experience full value, clean conversion moment
- Risk mitigation: Allow one-time 7-day extension for engaged users

**Option B: Credit-Based**
- Free: 50 tasks/month forever
- Paid: Unlimited tasks
- Why: Lets users try forever, convert when they hit limit
- Risk: May be enough for tiny operators, never convert

**Option C: Feature-Limited**
- Free: Basic features (simple Q&A, document generation)
- Paid: Advanced features (customer auto-updates, compliance tracking, integrations)
- Why: "Freemium" model, free users get value but want more
- Risk: Complex to communicate, may confuse

**Recommendation: Option A (time-limited)**
- Cleanest UX
- Forces decision (commitment point)
- Matches logistics industry buying behavior (prefer to pay for proven value)

### Current Thinking: $10/month (Validate)

**Why $10 works:**

### Tiered Pricing Structure (Recommended)

**Tier 1: "Solo" - $10/month**
- 1 user
- Unlimited tasks
- Basic integrations
- Email support
- **Target:** 1-3 truck owner-operators

**Tier 2: "Team" - $29/month**
- Up to 5 users (owner + dispatchers)
- Everything in Solo
- Priority support
- Custom workflows
- API access
- **Target:** 4-10 truck companies with small office

**Tier 3: "Fleet" - $79/month**
- Unlimited users
- Everything in Team
- Dedicated onboarding
- White-label option (bot appears as their company)
- Phone support
- **Target:** 10+ truck companies, freight brokers

### Anchoring Strategy

**On pricing page, show all three:**
```
Solo        Team           Fleet
$10/mo      $29/mo â­      $79/mo
            MOST POPULAR
```

**Psychological effect:**
- $10 looks like a steal (compared to $79)
- $29 seems like smart middle choice (gets 70% of customers)

**Alternative: Start simple, add tiers later**
- Launch with ONLY $10 tier
- After 10 customers, analyze usage patterns
- Add $29 tier for power users who want multi-user access

### Pricing Validation Tests

**Before launch, validate with warm lead:**
- Show $10 tier: "Would you pay this if the trial works?"
- Show $29 tier with extra features: "Would you upgrade for [feature]?"
- Ask: "What's the max you'd pay if this saved you 10 hours/week?"

**After 5 customers, test:**
- Offer $29 tier to 2-3 existing customers with extra features
- Track: Do they upgrade? What features matter?
- If no one upgrades â†’ $10 is the right price

## Risk Mitigation

**Risk: First customer doesn't convert**
- Mitigation: Extend trial, offer founding rate ($5/mo), or ask for detailed feedback to fix issues

**Risk: Logistics vertical too narrow**
- Mitigation: Test messaging with 2-3 other industries in parallel (construction, real estate, e-commerce)

**Risk: $10 too cheap to be sustainable**
- Mitigation: Launch $29 tier by Month 3, grandfather early customers at $10

**Risk: Free tier cannibalizes paid**
- Mitigation: Start with time-limited trial, add perpetual free tier only if needed for top-of-funnel

**Risk: Product isn't actually useful**
- Mitigation: Ship fast, get real feedback weekly, pivot features based on usage data
